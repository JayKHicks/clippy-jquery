clippy-jquery

clippy-jquery is a jQuery plugin that creates clippy objects to let your users copy arbitrary text from your web pages onto their clipboards.

Examples

Use the clippy widgets below to copy some sample text to your keyboard, and then paste them into the textarea on the right. Then click "View source" to see what makes it work. More documentation available here.
